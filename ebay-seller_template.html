2. what condition is it in?

Brand new: New, unused, and in perfect condition with nothing missing or damaged.

Like new: Shiny, undamaged, nothing missing, virtually new condition.

Very good: Doesn't look brand new but has no easily noticeable damage, very minimal identifying physical marks, very minimal physical wear and tear (You could give it to a good friend as a gift).

Good: Very minimal physical damage, minimal physical wear (You could use it yourself, but wouldn't necessarily give it as a gift).

Acceptable: Some physical damage, but integrity still intact (Item beaten up a bit but it works).

Unacceptable: Major physical damage.

Tested/Working: The item is used, has been tested for functionality, and appears to be in good working order.

Untested/Sold As-Is: The item was not tested by me. The item may or may not function as intended. This item is Sold As-Is with no expressed or implied warranty. Returns will not be accepted.
